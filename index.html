Dining

Ranked picks

Top pick

Zum Gemalten Haus

#1
Sachsenhausen · Apfelwein · Grune Sosse

A Frankfurt institution. Authentic apple wine tavern with warm service and the city's famous green sauce. This is exactly Jeff's lane: local, historic, unpretentious.

Metzgerei Zeissler

#2
Casual · Schnitzel · Lunch spot

Excellent lunch schnitzel in a no-frills setting. Ranks below Zum Gemalten Haus because it is lunch-focused and lacks the full cultural experience.

Kleinmarkthalle

#3
Market hall · Graze · Casual

Indoor market perfect for a midday graze. Multiple vendors mean variety, but it lacks the sit-down atmosphere of a proper tavern.
