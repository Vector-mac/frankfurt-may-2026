Walking nearby (within 1 mile of LUME)

  • Romer + Romerberg Square. Frankfurt's medieval town hall complex, used since the 15th century. ~1.2 km / 15 min walk east. The iconic gabled Romer on the west side of the square is the photo-stop. Source: jetsettingfools.com / viatravelers.com.
  • Mainufer (Main River walk). Immediately south of Bahnhofsviertel, ~5-min walk. Best views of the city skyline. Locals gather here evenings; couples stroll, runners loop, the river is the city's nightly living room. Source: marriott.com Frankfurt walking tours.
  • Stadel Museum. Across the Main River on the south bank, ~1 km / 15 min walk via Eiserner Steg or another bridge. 700 years of European art, neo-Renaissance building, one of the oldest museums in Germany. Indoor; perfect for Sat May 23 between Metallica nights. Matches your Nelson-Atkins playlist signal. Source: viatravelers.com.
  • Eiserner Steg (Iron Footbridge). Pedestrian-only bridge across the Main, links Bahnhofsviertel side to the Stadel/Museumsufer side. Worth the crossing at dusk for the photo.
  • Bahnhofsviertel street art walk. The neighborhood has a strong street art scene; an evening loop after dinner is on-brand. Source: meininger-hotels.com.
